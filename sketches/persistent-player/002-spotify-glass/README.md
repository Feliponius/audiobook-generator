## Variant: Spotify Glass

### Design stance
Make the player the star: richer, darker, more premium, and more obviously “audio-first” while still persisting across the app.

### Key choices
- Layout: dark glass shell with strong now-playing emphasis
- Typography: bolder, higher-contrast, more music-app-like hierarchy
- Color: charcoal, emerald, neon mint, and subtle purple
- Interaction: mini-player is more tactile; expanded sheet feels like a dedicated audio destination

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: perceived modernity, player presence, transport clarity
- Weak at: slightly less calm and slightly more visually assertive than the warm shell

### Best for
A listening-first experience where the player should feel premium and unmistakably persistent.
