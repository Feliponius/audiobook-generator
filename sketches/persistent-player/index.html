Persistent Player Mockups

Two mobile-first mockups for a stand-alone audiobook player that survives navigation. Both variants include a persistent mini-player, an expanded now-playing sheet, chapter context, and route changes that keep audio alive.

Variant 001

Play Books Shell

  • Warm, calm, Google Play Books-like shell
  • Persistent player bar above bottom nav
  • Reader route swaps the bottom bar for an in-reader dock
  • Good if you want “real app” comfort over audio-drama flash
Open variant
Variant 002

Spotify Glass

  • Darker, more album-centric and modern
  • Glass mini-player with bolder now-playing emphasis
  • Stronger chapter queue and transport controls
  • Good if you want the player to feel like the star feature
