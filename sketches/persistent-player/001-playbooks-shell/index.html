The science of availability

People assess the frequency of a class or the probability of an event by the ease with which instances or occurrences come to mind.

This is not only a judgment of the world. It is also a judgment of mental fluency. What comes to mind more easily often feels more common, more dangerous, or more true.

That is why a persistent audio dock matters here: the reading surface stays quiet, but listening never disappears.
