## Variant: Play Books Shell

### Design stance
Calm consumer-shell direction: treat playback as a natural extension of reading, not a separate power-user dashboard.

### Key choices
- Layout: warm library cards with a persistent bottom mini-player
- Typography: calm, readable, app-store/book-reader style hierarchy
- Color: parchment + evergreen + restrained blue accents
- Interaction: mini-player persists across tabs; reader hides the bottom bar and shows a slim in-reader audio dock

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: low-stress continuity, book-first feel, broad familiarity
- Weak at: less dramatic or "premium audio app" feeling than the darker option

### Best for
A reading-first product where the audiobook player should feel modern and helpful without dominating the app.
